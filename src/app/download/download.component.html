The Heart App

Requirements

In order to use the app and perform your own analysis the patient must have:

  • Android Device with Bluetooth 4+
  • Heart Rate Sensor
  • Internet Connection

Download

You can download the heart app by clicking this link.

Perform your own Analysis

The user must download and install the App, then the user must put on the heart rate sensor and launch the App, then the patient makes the app register and login, finally he selects the sensor connected in the App and the App begins to collect the data. The patient has to wear the sensor for 21 hours while performs his normal daily activities.
When the user has enough data to perform the analysis he goes to the diagnosis screen where he can execute the diagnosis generator process and see the results of the diagnosis.
